The K5UTD Amateur Radio Club maintains systems to serve local area hams and clubs.

**2m Repeater**

* 145.430MHz
* -600KHz offset
* pl110.9
* Located on the Tager Tower on UT Dallas campus.

K5RWK maintains this repeater on behalf of K5UTD.
